## Ingredients

- 1 lbs boneless, skinless chicken
- 1/3 c plain greek yogurt
- 1/4 c extra virgin olive oil
- 4 lemons
- 4-5 cloves of garlic
- 2 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
